Welcome to the Movie App! This guide explains how to use all major features, including logging in, browsing movies, viewing details, managing ratings, favorites, and watchlists, and using your profile.

Login

Logging In

  1. Open the application.
  2. Enter your email and password.
  3. Tap Login to continue.

Registering a New Account

  • New users must register through TMDB.
  • Tap Register with TMDB to create an account.

Forgot Password

  • Select Forgot Password to be redirected to TMDBâ€™s password recovery page.

Home Screen

The Home Screen serves as your main navigation hub.

Features

  • Browse trending, popular, top-rated, and upcoming movies or TV shows.
  • Scroll horizontally through carousels.
  • Tap any movie/show to open its detail page.
  • Access search and filtering (if available in your version).

Detail Page

The Detail Page contains full information about a selected movie or TV show.

Information Displayed

  • Title
  • Release Date
  • Synopsis

Actions Available

Rate a Movie

  • Select a score from 1 to 10.
  • Edit your rating at any time.
  • Remove your rating if desired.

Add to Favorites

  • Tap the Favorite icon to add or remove a title from your favorites list.

Add to Watchlist

  • Tap the Watchlist button to add or remove the title from your watchlist.

Favorites

  • Shows all the movies and shows you added to your watchlist.
  • Remove items anytime.

Recommendations

  • A personalized selection of at least 10 recommended movies/TV shows.
  • Recommendations improve based on your ratings, favorites, and watchlist activity.
